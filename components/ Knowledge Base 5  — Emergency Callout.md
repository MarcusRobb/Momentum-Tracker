# Knowledge Base — Emergency Callout (Zenith Roofing)

## What counts as an emergency?
An emergency is usually something like:
- Active roof leak (water coming in)
- Storm or hail damage
- Water ingress through ceilings
- Sudden roof damage creating risk to the home

## Do you offer emergency roof repairs?
Yes. We can escalate urgent issues as an emergency callout.

## How fast do you respond to emergency callouts?
We prioritise emergency calls and aim to respond within 24 hours (often sooner). Response timing can vary depending on time of day and availability.

## What’s the emergency callout fee?
If you request an emergency callout, there is a fixed callout fee of **$350 inc GST**.  
This covers the urgent attendance/callout itself. Any repair work required is assessed separately.

## Is the free inspection still free?
Yes. Standard inspections and quotes are free and no-obligation.  
The $350 inc GST fee only applies if you specifically request an emergency callout.

## What information do you need for an emergency callout?
To escalate an emergency callout, we need:
- Your name
- Best mobile number
- Full job address (street + suburb + postcode)
- A short description of the issue (e.g., “water dripping in lounge after storm”)

## What if it’s after hours?
We’ll capture your details immediately and send them through to the team.  
If it’s late at night, the office may respond when they’re open or as soon as someone is available.

If there’s immediate danger to people or property, contact emergency services.
